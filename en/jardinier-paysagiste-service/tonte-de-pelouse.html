6 Lawn Mowing

Want a lawn as beautiful as your neighbor's… without spending all your weekends on it? We’ve got it covered for you! With our lawn mowing service, say goodbye to battles with your mower and trips to the garden center. Our experts take care of everything: from perfect cutting to fertilization, including soil aeration. And no matter the whims of the Belgian climate, we have the solution for green, dense grass all year round. In short, relax, we’ve got the green thumb!

  • Your lawn deserves the best, not your Saturdays.
  • Let the pros handle the mower, you keep the relaxation.
  • A perfect lawn, with no effort on your part.

Services:

Our lawn mowing service goes well beyond simple cutting. We start by assessing the condition of your lawn to determine the necessary care: selection of suitable seeds, targeted fertilization, and weed control. Then, we perform regular and meticulous mowing, adjusting the height according to the season and type of grass. We also carry out soil aeration to avoid compaction and promote better nutrient absorption. Our comprehensive approach guarantees a lush, thick, and easy-to-maintain lawn.

  • Regular mowing for always impeccable grass.
  • Custom fertilization for vibrant green.
  • Soil aeration for a healthy lawn.
  • Weed management for clean grass.
  • Cutting adjustment according to season and type of lawn.
  • Turnkey service for a garden that stands out.
